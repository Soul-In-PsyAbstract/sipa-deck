Aesthetic Direction

Timeless over trendy. The hlvrn visual system borrows from precision-grade cosmetics and clinical supplements — not sports performance. Every element earns its place: no gradients, no pattern fill, no superfluous detail. The packaging should feel as considered at 10cm as at 10 metres.

Stage 01

File review

Stage 02

Refinement pass

Improve hierarchy, spacing, visual weight. Develop stick pack system to match pouch.
