AI-Powered Content Workflow

1

Intake (30 min/week)

I brief the AI system with your latest services, any news, and audience signals from the previous week's analytics.

2

Draft Generation (automated)

SIPA OS generates 8–10 draft posts per week using Claude + mental health content models. All drafts follow clinical tone guidelines.

3

Human Review (15 min)

I review, refine, and approve 4–5 posts for the week. Anything that doesn't pass the "would a clinician cringe?" test is rewritten.

4

Scheduled & Tracked

Posts go out via automated scheduler. Engagement, reach, and inquiry uplift tracked weekly. Strategy adjusts monthly.
