The Concept

Science they buried.
A signal that won't stay quiet.

LAST LIGHT is a six-part limited series following Dr. Mara Voss, a radio telescope engineer at a remote Chilean observatory. During a routine calibration sweep, she intercepts an anomalous signal from the Cygnus X-3 binary system — a dying neutron star 37,000 light-years away.


The pattern is mathematical. Intentional. Repeating. Within 18 hours of her initial report, the observatory receives a visit from a delegation she was never told to expect. By hour 24, the signal is classified.


Mara has one backup drive. And she trusts no one.
