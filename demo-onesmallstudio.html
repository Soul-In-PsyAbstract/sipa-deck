How it works

From room upload to lighting brief in 90 seconds

Your existing AI component analyses the scene. LUMA wraps it in a production SaaS — auth, billing, team management, and a client-ready export.

STEP 01

Upload the room

Designer uploads photos or a floor plan. Claude identifies materials, proportions, existing light sources, and window orientation.

STEP 02

AI lighting analysis

The model returns fixture placement recommendations, colour temperature ranges, and layer strategies — with reasoning your clients can read.

STEP 03

Export the brief

Download a formatted lighting brief as PDF. Share a client-facing link. Save to project history. Every analysis is stored and searchable.
