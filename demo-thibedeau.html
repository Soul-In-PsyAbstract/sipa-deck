Automation Design
Membership Automation Workflow — Visual Map

What happens automatically from the moment someone joins, so the program runs without manual follow-up.

Step 1
New member signs up
Payment confirmed, account created, tagged by membership tier.
→
Step 2
Welcome email sequence
Immediate access email + 3-part welcome series over the first week.
→
Step 3
Drip-released onboarding modules
Foundational resources unlocked on a schedule so new members aren't overwhelmed on day one.
→
Step 4
Webinar reminder automation
Auto-enrolled in upcoming webinars with reminder emails at 48h, 24h, and 1h before.
→
Step 5
Renewal reminder
Automated renewal sequence starts 30 days out, with a direct line to Catherine if a member has questions.
Full Delivery Scope
What Gets Built (Natively in Kajabi)
Membership site architecture — home, library, webinars, account

Drip-release schedule for onboarding modules
Webinar reminder automation (48h / 24h / 1h before)
Renewal reminder automation sequence
Branding pass — colors, fonts, logo placement across the portal
Mobile-responsive review across all member-facing pages
Walkthrough handoff + short async support window
